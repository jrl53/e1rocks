Ladies and Gentlemen of the best section INSEAD has ever seen, SECTION 1!!!

First a brief explanation of champagne fines. Essentially they are small fines that serve two purposes: 1) to ensure certain behaviours in class are either encouraged or discouraged, and 2) to allow the section to build a fund that is dedicated exclusively to supporting Section 1 events (and by supporting, we of course mean providing lots of booze and tasty snacks)


The Fines

The Sleeper

€999 per nod
  • If you are caught sleeping
  • Picture required

The Smelly Feet

€499 per instance
  • If caught with your shoes off
  • Picture required

The Late Doubter

€499 per question
  • If asks question in the last 5 min
  • Notified in Whatsapp

The Mr. Obvious

€499 per instance
  • If starts question with:
  • "I have a question .."

The Uber Participant

€499 per class
  • If askes more than 5 questions in one class
  • Notified in Whatsapp

Most Useless Question

  • Voted in Whatsapp

Best Contribution

1 beer per week
  • At the end of each week, the academic and social reps will vote for the comment that did the least to advance classroom discussion / dumbest comment
